Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-27

Overview

StrongPass.pro is designed to protect your privacy. Password generation happens entirely in your browser; we do not receive your passwords or passphrases.

Data collection

  • Passwords & passphrases: Not collected, not stored, not transmitted.
  • Personal data: We do not require accounts or personal information to use the tool.

Cookies and tracking

By default, we do not use cookies or tracking scripts. If we add analytics or advertising in the future, we will update this policy and provide appropriate consent controls.

Third‑party links

Our site may link to third‑party websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

Security

We use the Web Crypto API to generate randomness locally. Always keep your device and browser updated.

Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
